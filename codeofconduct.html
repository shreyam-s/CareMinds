CareMinds Code of Conduct

At CareMinds, we are dedicated to fostering a compassionate, supportive, and safe environment for all users. Our community values openness, empathy, and respect for every individual. To ensure a positive experience for everyone, we ask all participants to adhere to the following guidelines:

1. Respectful Communication

Be kind, considerate, and respectful in your interactions with others. Avoid offensive, harmful, or demeaning language, including hate speech, discriminatory remarks, or bullying of any kind. Respect differing opinions and experiences.

2. Confidentiality

Do not share private or personal information about others without consent. Respect the privacy of all community members. Avoid discussing sensitive topics in a manner that could expose someone's identity without their permission.

3. Non-Professional Advice

CareMinds is a platform for mental health support, not professional therapy or diagnosis. Refrain from offering medical advice unless you are a licensed mental health professional. Encourage others to seek help from certified professionals when necessary.

4. Supportive Environment

Offer encouragement and empathy to others who may be struggling with mental health challenges. Avoid judgmental or dismissive comments. Refrain from glorifying or promoting harmful behaviors (e.g., self-harm, substance abuse).

5. Harassment and Abuse

Harassment, stalking, threats, or any form of abuse will not be tolerated. If you feel unsafe or witness inappropriate behavior, please report it to the CareMinds team immediately.

6. Trigger Warnings

Use trigger warnings for discussions involving sensitive topics such as trauma, self-harm, or suicide. Respect the emotional boundaries of others and avoid graphic descriptions of harmful behaviors.

7. No Promotion of Harmful Content

Do not share content that promotes self-harm, eating disorders, or other dangerous behaviors. Content that glorifies or normalizes harmful actions is strictly prohibited.

8. Reporting Violations

If you observe any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please notify us via our reporting system. All reports will be handled with confidentiality and care.
